Thesis: Information Foraging Theory
In my ongoing thesis (estimated completion, July 2018), I am exploring Information Foraging Theory. We hunt for information in the exact same way that we've evolved to hunt for food. How long do we spend in one place before we move on? How do we estimate how much food that place will have? If we understand that human behavior better, maybe we can make the Software Engineering process better. Can we make developing more efficient by making good information easier to reach, identify, and navigate? Especially for end users—not full-time programmers—this is a particularly relevant question. Keep your eye on this space for more details as progress progresses. Image Source
